Rachael's 12 Days of Christmas 2020!


Hello, and welcome to your 12 days of Christmas celebration!

I know what you're thinking. What is this thing?

Well... I compiled all the Christmasy things I could think of, all the activities, aesthetics, feelings of the holiday, and split them into 12 days of awesome for you to enjoy!

You're never too old, too out of school, too devoid of a niece or nephew, or anything else, to be able to enjoy all the holidays.

I hope these 12 days fill you up with some joy and some holly jolly good feels. After all, that's all that really matters.

Click on the day to go to that day's activities! Scroll down to see the things you should check out, but don't read past the day!
